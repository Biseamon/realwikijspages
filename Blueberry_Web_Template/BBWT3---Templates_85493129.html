Blueberry Group : BBWT3 - Templates

Created by Jason Cozza on Sep 21, 2020

Templates provide the structure of the client application HTML, as opposed to themes which provide the colour schemes.

Overview

BBWT3 is currently configured to use the Ultima template from PrimeFaces.

Template Comparison

PrimeFaces offer a number of templates for PrimeNG applications.

A comparison of compatible templates (compatible in the sense that it should be easiest to drop in a replacement) are detailed below;

Template

Current Price

Notes

Ultima$790
  • Current template used by BBWT3.
  • Provides twelve themes with varying colour schemes.
Poseidon$390
  • Looks similar to Ultima with a much cheaper license.
  • Three menu modes.
  • Eight themes included, changes colour highlights on some components.
Paradise$390
  • Flat design compared to Ultima but shares a similar layout.
Olympia$390
  • Even more flat design than Paradise.
Morpheus$390
  • Clean design, quite flat.

All of the above templates are built with CSS3, HTML5, SASS, and are mobile and touch responsive. 

For Project Managers

  • Templates can be switched out to provide an entirely new application layout.
  • Some templates may offer better touch/mobile support than others, if the application is mobile-heavy.

For Marketing

  • Switch out templates to get the best support for your platform.
  • The application can get a fresh look by updating the template to more modern design standards.
