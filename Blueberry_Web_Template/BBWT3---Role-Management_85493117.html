Blueberry Group : BBWT3 - Role Management

Created by Jason Cozza on Sep 21, 2020

Overview

Any individual user may belong to many roles and/or many groups. A role permits a user to access certain types of data, while a group permits access to certain data.

  • View and edit users role.
  • Ability to assign multiple roles to an individual user.

Test Case

  1. An existing user has been promoted.
  2. They now need further permission on the web application.
  3. Admin accesses role management feature and grants permissions.

For Testers

  1. From the BBWT3 menu click on "Operational Admin".
  2. From the drop down menu click on "Users".
  3. Click on the pencil icon next to the user you wish to edit.
  4. Displayed will be "Basic details" as indicated by the tab at the top.
  5. At the top click on the tab "Roles".
  6. Select by clicking the checkbox which role you wish to give the user.

For Project Managers

  • Generally the available roles break down like this:
    • Super Admin: Intended for Blueberry support team. Has wide ranging privileges e.g. ability to edit site's connection to email server, change site colour scheme etc.
    • System Admin: Intended for the client's internal support team. Has the ability to manage users but not have access to security features.
    • Company Admin: Intended for business-to-business sites where the end users belong to sizeable organisations. Has the ability to control which of its own staff have access to certain features and/or data.
    • Manager: An example of a role that a Company Admin could create. Used for demonstration purposes on BBWT3 demo site.
  • Roles can be customised to the clients requirements.

For Marketing

  • Ability to compartmentalise users.
  • Custom group's and roles.
