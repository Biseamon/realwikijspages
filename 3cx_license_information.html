The license key itself is stored in BitWarden in the note 3CX License Key.

 

Renewal process:

  1. Log into the 3CX customer portal (see BitWarden).
  2. Navigate to Keys.
  3. Click the renew button.
  4. Fill out the details as required.

Once the purchase is confirmed, navigate to our 3CX installation license page and click Refresh License Key Information.
