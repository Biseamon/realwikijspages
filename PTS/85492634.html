Blueberry Group : What is included in the Customer Portal? 

Created by Jason Cozza on Aug 24, 2020

This feature allows customers to log in and review the status of their project - they login through the same URL as BB employees (pts.bbconsult.co.uk)

Customers will have specific permissions set that allow them limited access to PTS - these can be customised as necessary to give more or less access on a customer by customer basis.

Features that the customer does not have access to will simply be hidden from them.
