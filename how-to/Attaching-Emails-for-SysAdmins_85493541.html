Blueberry Group : Attaching Emails for SysAdmins

Created by Jason Cozza on Nov 24, 2020

If you are having issues with mails, SysAdmins will need a copy of the bounce / original email so that they can see the headers.

To do this:

  1. Open a new mail to send to sysadmins@bbconsult.co.uk
  2. Find the bounce / original email in your Inbox.
  3. Drag and drop the bounce / original email from your Inbox on to the blank new mail to SysAdmins - your bounce / original email will now be added to the new email as an attachment.
