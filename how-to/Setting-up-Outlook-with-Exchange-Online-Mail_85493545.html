Blueberry Group : Setting up Outlook with Exchange Online Mail

Created by Jason Cozza on Nov 24, 2020

The process is largely the same as setting up Outlook with our on-premises Exchange server.

Webmail can be accessed at 365.bbconsult.co.uk.

Step-by-step guide

  1. Close Outlook
  2. Remove your current Outlook profile by going to Control Panel > Mail > Profiles
  3. Start Outlook
  4. Give your new profile a name

  5. Fill out the Auto Account Setup in the following manner:

    Your name: Your first and last name
    E-mail Address: firstname.lastname@bbconsult.co.uk (or blueberrysystems, bbsoftware etc depending where you work)
    Password: Your password


  6. If you entered @365.bbconsult.co.uk as part of your email earlier, you will be prompted to enter your username and password. In this case, use firstname.lastname@bbconsult/blueberrysystems/bbsoftware.co.uk as your username, and your password as normal. Tick "Remember my credentials".
  7. You will be prompted to restart Outlook when finished.


8. If you want to create a Blueberry signature, please see our Blueberry Signature Creator tool.
