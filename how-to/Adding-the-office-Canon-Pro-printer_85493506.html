Blueberry Group : Adding the office Canon Pro printer

Created by Jason Cozza on Nov 24, 2020

Step-by-step guide


  1. Open the File Explorer on your computer
  2. Open the Network folder
  3. Double click on PRINT-SERVER
  4.  Double click on the Canon printer

The printer will now be installed on your computer. You may need to restart any application you want to print from, or at least re-open any printer selection dialogs.
