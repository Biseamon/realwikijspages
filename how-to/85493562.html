Blueberry Group : Connecting to Blueberry's customer FTP server 

Created by Jason Cozza on Nov 24, 2020

Blueberry recommends using FileZilla to connect to the FTP server.

 

This guide is for connecting to the customer only FTP server. For the internal/employee FTP server, please see Connecting to Blueberry's FTP server.

Please contact the system administrators for customer FTP details, or if you require a new customer FTP account.

 

 

Step-by-step guide

 

  1. Open FileZilla and open the Site Manager.
     
  1. Click on New Site and fill out the details on the right hand side as follows:

    Host: ftp-cust.bbconsult.co.uk
    Port: 21
    Protocol: FTP
    Encryption: Require explicit FTP over TLS
    Logon type: Normal
    Username: Customer account
    Password: Customer password
  1. Click on Transfer Settings and set the Transfer mode to Passive
  2. Click Connect. The site will be saved, and you will now be connected to the customer FTP server.
