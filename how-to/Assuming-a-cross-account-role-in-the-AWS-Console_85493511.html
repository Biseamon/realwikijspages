Blueberry Group : Assuming a cross-account role in the AWS Console

Created by Jason Cozza on Nov 24, 2020

You may need to assume a cross-account role from your main login in Blueberry's AWS account. This is considered more secure than having different, isolated logins across AWS accounts.

Step-by-step guide


  1. Login to your Blueberry AWS account as normal using this link.
  2. Click your account name in the top-right, and select Switch Role.
  3. Follow the instructions and fill out with information given to you by your administrator.
  4.  For example for PKF Cooper Parry customer AWS account you need to set it up like it is shown below:

You may also construct a URL for your convenience to assume the role i.e. https://signin.aws.amazon.com/switchrole?roleName=ROLE_NAME&account=ACCOUNT_ID_OR_ALIAS - substitute the role name and account id/alias as appropriate.
