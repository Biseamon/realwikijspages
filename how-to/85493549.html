Blueberry Group : Viewing somebody else's calendar in Outlook

Created by Jason Cozza on Nov 24, 2020

This guide will allow you to view the Exchange calendars of other people within Blueberry.

Step-by-step guide

  1. Switch to the Calendar tab in Outlook.
  2. Click Open Calendar and then Fron Address Book.
  3. Search for or click on the user(s) whose calendar you want to view and click the Calendar -> button.
  4. Click OK when done.
