Blueberry Group : BB Support Plugin (Blueberry Outloot) 

Created by Jason Cozza on Nov 24, 2020

An Outlook plugin designed to help Blueberry's communication being more efficient. It includes the following features:
 

  • PTS Helper to help you creating tickets in the PTS based on a customer email.
  • Newsletter Template to create quickly your own newsletter. An easy way to send updates to the customers about their ongoing projects with us.
  • Watchdog to help you answering your emails in a certain delay.
