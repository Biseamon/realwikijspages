Blueberry Group : BB Support Plugin (Blueberry Outloot)

Created by Jason Cozza on Nov 24, 2020

An Outlook plugin designed to help Blueberry's communication being more efficient. It includes the following features:

  • PTS Helper to help you creating tickets in the PTS based on a customer email.
  • Newsletter Template to create quickly your own newsletter. An easy way to send updates to the customers about their ongoing projects with us.
  • Watchdog to help you answering your emails in a certain delay.

Brief History

Developed by Victor Simart.
This project started a simple request of some people in the office asking for a way to be warned when a customer email has not been answered within 15 minutes. Then, if a project manager notices one, it would mean support team is struggling and some of them may help us saying “I’m on this one”.
A few time after, Victor has been asked to create another plugin to help us with PTS which website’s a bit slow, so he added a simple interface to create these emails easily, based on customer emails.
Victor also has another feature to add, but he's not on it yet at all. He plans to try to do it as soon as possible.
Victor needed some webservice for the PTS feature as it needs to get the list of projects from the PTS. Talking with Oliver G., Martin G., Alex V. […] they wanted him to merge all of his features within their existing add-in. The thing is that they are using a huge library and merging both would make it all even bigger and slower and people here just  wanted one simple feature.
So, Victor is developing his own version at the moment and will try to merge his features with their at the very end.
