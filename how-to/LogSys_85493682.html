Blueberry Group : LogSys

Created by Jason Cozza on Nov 24, 2020

LogSys is a simple, easy to use tool for reporting problems in software. It saves you time by automatically collecting information from your computer and it knows who supports the software. It consists of a "server" part (a service that traps messages sent by the applications) and a client library that is used by these applications.
