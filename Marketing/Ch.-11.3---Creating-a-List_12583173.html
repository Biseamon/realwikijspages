Blueberry : Ch. 11.3 - Creating a List

Created by Stelios Dritsas, last modified on Jul 04, 2016

To create a new list, look towards the upper right-hand side of your page and click the blue button called ‘New list’.

  1. From the opened page look towards the left hand side and click ‘Name list’.

  2. Enter a name for your list and choose whether to have a smart or static list by selecting the appropriate radio button, then click the blue button for ‘Save’.

  3. Also from this left hand side you’ll also see options for ‘Segment contact by’, which include:

    1. Contact property

    2. List membership

    3. Form submission

    4. Email

    5. Page view

    6. Event

    7. Workflow status

    8. Call-to-Action

  4. Please note, different segmenting options will require different types of information, so it’s best to have a clear understanding of who you’re trying to segment.

  5. From these options choose your segmenting parameters:

    1. In my case I chose ‘Form Submission’.

       6. I then click the dropdown menu for ‘any form’ and click the entry for ‘Register for CMM’.

       7. This action then changes the right hand side of my page to a preview of those contacts who’ve completed this form

       8. Potentially, I could choose to segment my list further and only show contacts who’ve completed my form via specific pages, by clicking the ‘any page’ dropdown menu and selecting the related page.

       9. Additionally, by clicking the ‘Refine by…’ button I can segment my contacts even further by the following options.

    1. No filter

    2. Rolling dates

    3. On or before date

    4. On or after date

    5. Between dates

    6. Number of times

      10. When I’ve chosen my segmentation options, I click ‘Done’, and am redirected back to enter further options.

      11. From here, by clicking ‘and’ you can add additional segmenting steps to what you’ve already done.

      12. Or by clicking ‘or…’ you can additional segmenting steps alongside what you’ve already done.

      13. When you’ve completed all you segmenting options click ‘Save’ and then press ‘Back to lists’ to finish.
