To access Website pages from the dashboard, navigate to Content > Website. Please note, you can also read HS’s own guidance on this subject here.

The presented website dashboard will have opened under the ‘Manage’ tab, which displays all the currently available website pages (both published and drafted) for the default domain - displayed towards the top-left via a drop-down menu (below).

You can change the displayed domain by clicking this drop-down menu and choosing your preferred website - where it will show its published pages with a small green circle and drafted pages with a small small grey circle.

To manage these pages there are some hidden options available to the left of the ‘created date’ for a website entry, hover your cursor over this section to view buttons for Edit, Clone, and More.

You can segment these pages by ‘campaign’ by selecting the ‘All campaigns’ drop-down menu and then clicking the campaign of your choice (below). In addition, clicking the ‘Analyse’ link will now show the associated metric data for these pages (below).

How to change a Website Page into a Landing Page:

To change a website page into a landing page (for whatever reason), click the dropdown menu for ‘more’ (located towards the far right) and choose ‘Move to landing pages’.

A popup will then appear and state a condition of the move, if okay, press the ‘Move to Landing Pages’ button to finish.
