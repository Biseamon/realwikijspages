To access Reports / Sources navigate to Reports and click > Sources. Please note, you can read HS’s own guidance on this subject here.

The presented page shows a table of source data broken down by a specific web domain and date range – with different colours representing various channels (as shown below).

To break this data down further, click the button for 'Standard / Cumulative' and the drop down menu for 'Visits > Contacts / Customers’ as needed.

To discover more information on a particular source, click any of the highlighted options for Organic Search', 'Referrals' etc., to see what channels / entries contribute towards that source’s result.

To choose a specific domain, click the drop down menu for ‘All domains’ and choose your preferred domain. However, if your domain does not exist, then you’ll need to contact the person currently managing HubSpot (it’s not an easy fix).

In addition, to find out which source has created the most contacts, and to find out who these contacts are - click the highlighted stats in the ‘Contacts’ column.

This information is particularly useful for any monthly statistics reporting - which you can learn how to do here.
