Blueberry : Ch. 7.8 - Review

Created by Stelios Dritsas on Jul 01, 2016

By opening the Review tab (located towards the top of the page) you will open the page for ‘Review before you send’ which checks your emails for any inconsistencies.

The page will be split into three different sections.

  1. Finishing touches: Steps from previous topics that need completion

  2. Suggestions: Non-compulsory steps that need attention. However, its best practice to complete these steps anyway.

  3. Done: Completed steps (steps from ‘finishing touches’ will be moved here after completion)

This section is fairly straightforward – complete as necessary. Once you’ve completed these steps, press save and proceed to the ‘Send or schedule’ tab.

However, if you email is for Automation (workflow use) then you will not have this step, instead there should be a blue button called ‘save for automation’ towards the top right. Click it, to finish your automated email.
