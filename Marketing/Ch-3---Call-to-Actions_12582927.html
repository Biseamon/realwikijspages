To access ‘Call-to-Actions’ from the dashboard, navigate to Content > Calls-to-Action. Please note, you can also read HS’s own guidance on this subject here.

The presented CTA dashboard will display a list of existing CTA’s and accompanying performance results, these results are segmented into the following categories and are shown in the image directly below.

From left to right these categories denote:

  • Status: The ‘zig-zag’ status symbol will show CTA timeline activity.

  • CTA: The name and look of the CTA.

  • Updated: When the CTA was last updated.

  • Views: How many times a visitor has seen it.

  • Views to Clicks: The percentage of views to clicks (best measure of performance).

  • Clicks: How many times a visitor has clicked it.

  • Clicks to Submissions: The percentage of clicks to forms associated with that page.

You can segment the CTA view by clicking on these column headers, but the ‘Views to Clicks’ metric is probably the best measure of CTA performance.

The associated content of these CTA’s can also be viewed by clicking on the icons to the far right-hand side (shown above). These are separated into the categories of Pages, Landing Pages, Emails and Blog Posts.
