Blueberry : Ch. 7.9 - Send or Schedule

Created by Stelios Dritsas on Jul 01, 2016

From the Send or schedule tab (located towards the top of the page) you can finalise the sending of your email. Please note, this option only appears if you’re sending your email to contacts, and not for automation.

The process from here is very simple, either choose ‘Send now’ followed by clicking the blue button called ‘Send’ towards the top-right of the page to finish. Or, choose ‘Schedule it for later’ followed by your details and then clicking the blue button called ‘Schedule’ towards the top-right of the page to finish.
