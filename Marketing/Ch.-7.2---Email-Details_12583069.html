Blueberry : Ch. 7.2 - Email Details

Created by Stelios Dritsas, last modified on Jul 01, 2016

Clicking within the ‘email details’ box (below) will expand it for additional information entry.

  1. From name: Use the drop-down menu to enter your sender’s name > add a contact by clicking add/manage.

  2. From email address: Use the drop-down menu to enter your email address of your sender > add an address by clicking add/manage.

  3. Subject line: Enter your subject line

  4. Preview text: Enter your preview text

When preparing this information try to use a real persons ‘from’ name person and email address, as opposed to a pseudo name or ‘team’ message. This makes the message much more personal and engaging, but isn’t always necessary for routine and automated responses that wouldn’t look genuine anyway.

For your subject line, make it attention grabbing but also nice and concise, this is what will make someone open your email. Whilst you preview text can just be an adjusted first sentence from your main email, but edit as necessary. When finished click done.
