Please Note: The following sections for ‘Enrolment and Actions’ might prove difficult for those not familiar with HS, because they will present the user with a significant amount of unfamiliar data. As previously mentioned, you can make these sections easier by having an already prepared flow-chart, and a clear understanding of which HS contact properties pull in the right data. For instructions see chapter 10.1.

When presented with the main workflow creator, your first step is to choose whether workflow contacts should be enrolled automatically or manually.

For a manual enrolment, you can simply add contacts ‘ad-hoc’ by using the Enrol link towards the upper right-hand side of the page.

For an automatic enrolment, follow the on-screen instructions and choose from a list of 8 contact segments that will accurately target your audience (below), and follow the onscreen instructions.
