To access Workflows from the main dashboard, navigate to Contacts > Workflows. Please note, you can also read HS’s own guidance on this subject here.

When opened, the presented dashboard will display every workflow currently created.

Workflows with a green dot indicate that they’re active, while workflows with a grey dot indicate that they’re inactive.

From here you can sort your view of these workflows by selecting either the Name, Total Contacts, Active Contacts, Last Modified, and Author (all shown below).

For clarification, the difference between Total Contacts and Active Contacts, is that the former includes all the contacts who’ve been entered into the workflow (including those who’ve completed it), against the latter of active contacts currently within it.

Furthermore, you can easily segment these workflows by selecting any of the drop-down menus for ‘All Workflows’, ‘All Personas’, and ‘All Authors’ (shown below).
