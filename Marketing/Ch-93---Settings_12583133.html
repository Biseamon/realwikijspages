Blueberry : Ch. 9.3 - Settings

Created by Stelios Dritsas on Jul 04, 2016

Clicking the ‘Settings’ tab towards the top of the page and enter the following information:

  1. Page name: Enter a name for internal reference (this will not be published)

  2. Page title: This is the bold text that shows up search engine result pages

    1. Tips for making your page rank well

      1. Honestly reflect the content of your page (or users just click away!)

      2. Don’t duplicate or use text similar to other pages

      3. Use targeted keywords, but use them towards the beginning of your title rather than the end – search engines place more value on earlier words

      4. Be brief, keep you text to around 5 words or <4 keywords, as the maximum length of characters shown for pages titles is 70

      5. Make the page title is actionable and appealing

  3. Page URL: A URL is automatically assigned using your page title, but you can manually edit it by clicking inside the text box - the shorter and more optimized the better here

    1. Domain: To the left of your URL box the domain is also displayed, click it to choose the right domain for your landing page

  4. Meta description: Add a Meta description to drive search engines to your page, but keep it under 160 characters, use keywords, and make sure it’s unique and actionable like a call-to-action.

  5. Campaign: Select a campaign or create a new one with ‘Add new’
