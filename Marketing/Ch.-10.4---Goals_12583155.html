Blueberry : Ch. 10.4 - Goals

Created by Stelios Dritsas on Jul 04, 2016

Next you have the optional action of setting-up a workflow ‘Goal’. Which allows you to benchmark a list of HS contact properties that will automatically un-enrol an active contact when met (regardless of stage).

To do this, select the link called ‘Set a goal’ enter the contact properties for unenrollement, and finish by clicking the blue ‘set goal’ button (below).

Those that do become unenrolled are then counted at the top of the workflow, and help you to see how many contacts have completed the workflow. However this is an optional step, and don’t feel obliged to complete this, as most of the workflows for CMM do not include a goal (DD).
