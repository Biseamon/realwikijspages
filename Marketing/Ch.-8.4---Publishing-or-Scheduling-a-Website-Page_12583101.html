Blueberry : Ch. 8.4 - Publishing or Scheduling a Website Page

Created by Stelios Dritsas on Jul 01, 2016

Before completing this next section, make sure that all your orange to-do’s’ in ‘Optimise’ under ‘Edit’ are now green, and that your additional information under ‘Settings’ is also complete.

Publish or schedule your landing page under the third main subsection ‘Publish or Schedule’:

Select either the ‘Publish now’ option, to publish the post immediately, with up to a 15 minute wait time. Or select the ‘Schedule for later’ option and follow the presented instructions to schedule your message at the desired time.
