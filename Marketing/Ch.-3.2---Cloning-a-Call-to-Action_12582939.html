Blueberry : Ch. 3.2 - Cloning a Call-to-Action

Created by Stelios Dritsas, last modified on Jun 30, 2016

It’s much faster to clone a CTA rather than creating a new design, but you must remember to change the text and destination link.

You can clone a CTA from the dashboard by clicking the gear and arrow icon located towards the far right-hand side (shown below), and choosing the ‘Clone’ option.

In the presented pop-up and information tab, change the CTA’s ‘Name’, ‘Link to’, and ‘Redirect to page’ options. Next, in the design tab, change the text within the editor to the chosen CTA copy, then finish by clicking the blue button ‘Update CTA’ in the bottom right.

However, keep in mind that the measured success of a CTA is only relevant within the context of the page and where it’s used. As such, reusing a successful CTA across a different page will not guarantee the same level of effectiveness.
