Blueberry : Ch. 2.2 - Uploadng an Image

Created by Unknown User (mct) on Feb 28, 2016

Before uploading your image have it prepared with the correct file name.

Please note, if you’re uploading an image in preparation for a blog post, make sure the image’s file name includes your primary search term - which counts as your image’s ‘Alt Tag’ within HubSpot optimisation.

To upload a new image look towards the upper-right hand corner of your dashboard (File Manager or from the appropriate image folder) and click the blue button called ‘Upload Files…’, or alternatively, drag the chosen file into the section called ‘Drag files here’ (shown below).

 

From here choose your specific image (enter a name if needed) and click open - not necessary if you've used the ‘Drag files here’ option.

To move an image into the correct folder, click the ‘Gear’ icon (below) towards the right-hand side of the image entry, click 'Move' and choose the appropriate folder for your image.
