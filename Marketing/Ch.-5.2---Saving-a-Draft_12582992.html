Blueberry : Ch. 5.2 - Saving a Draft

Created by Stelios Dritsas on Jun 30, 2016

For reference, to continue editing a blog post at a later time, hit the ‘Save’ button towards the top right-hand corner of the page. Now, the page can be closed without any loss of information.

However, to access a draft later on, navigate back to the Blogging dashboard, and look towards the upper left-hand side to a section labelled ‘drafts’. Click through to resume your progress.
