Blueberry : Ch. 12.3 - Grouping Modules

Created by Stelios Dritsas on Jul 05, 2016

  1. To group modules together press the ‘Group…’ button and select your modules in rectangular groupings.

  2. Incompatible groupings will be highlighted in red, while compatible groupings will be highlighted in green.

  3. When you have a compatible group click the blue ‘create group’ button to finish.

  4. Please note, you can also make grouped modules ‘global’
