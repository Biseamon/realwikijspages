Blueberry : Ch. 10.2 - Creating a Workflow

Created by Stelios Dritsas on Jul 04, 2016

To create a new Workflow, look towards the upper right-hand side of the page and click the blue button called ‘Create new workflow’.

From the presented pop-up, choose a name for your workflow and a structure ‘type’ from either Standard, Fixed date, or Property-based.

  1. A Standard workflow is triggered when a contact enrols after performing an action such as joining a HS list, visiting a page, or submitting a form.

  2. A Fixed date workflow means that contacts are enrolled based around the same criteria as a Standard workflow, but it’s triggered against a calendar date.

  3. A Property based workflow means that contacts are enrolled based around the same criteria as a Standard and Fixed date workflow, but it’s triggered against a custom contact date property.
