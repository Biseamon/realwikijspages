Blueberry : Ch. 7.3 - Left Hand Side Menu

Created by Stelios Dritsas on Jul 01, 2016

On the left-hand side of the page you will see icons for the following:

Descending in order:

  1. Preview: View the page as your audience via several device types

  2. Send test email: Send a test email to yourself and view how it’s received

  3. Test in email clients: Test how you email is viewed within a range of email clients

  4. Optimise: Complete the optimisation sections suggested, but don’t worry about missing CTA’s as they’re not always necessary.

  5. Edit modules: A different way to enter your email information, you can select between all the different modules of your email and edit or adjust them as necessary.

  6. A/B test: Create two versions of your email to test what factors engage your audience more.
