Blueberry : Ch. 15 - Adding to the Navigation Menu

Created by Unknown User (mct) on Jul 07, 2016

Contents 


To add to the navigation menu, navigate to Content and click > Content Settings. Please note, you can read HS’s own guidance on this subject here.

Note, for this process to work, you must’ve published the page you wish to link to - if you haven't done this, go back and publish your page.

 

From the opened page, look to the LHS and click a link called ‘Advanced Menus’ > then from the drop down menu for 'default', choose the domain you wish to add to.

Find the page or subpage you wish to follow-on from > click the ‘Add page’ button > and choose your URL (below) > then click 'Choose page'.

Click the ‘Link Settings’ (gear) icon to choose your menu's label name > then click 'Save'.

You can now publish or save the page as a draft.

 

However, be aware that if you don’t publish your changes, they’ll remain (saved) within the same draft until someone else revisits them – which can be potentially dangerous. As another user could publish their own work and not be aware of your  unfinished changes. Therefore, make sure any changes you make are fully completed, and not left saved within a draft
