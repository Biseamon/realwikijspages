Blueberry : Ch. 6.1 - Inbox

Created by Stelios Dritsas, last modified on Jul 01, 2016

The Inbox page provides notifications on what new social interactions your accounts have had (like Twitter and Facebook); displayed through both a numeric counter next to the Inbox link, and text updates from the main-feed.

However, the main feed will only display updates from one account at a time. To see what interactions different accounts have had, select the ‘All accounts’ drop-down menu from the right-hand side of the page, and choose your preferred account.

In addition, to change between the types of interactions displayed, select the ‘All item types’ menu (to the right of All accounts), and choose between the new options of Conversations, New followers, and Stream matches.
