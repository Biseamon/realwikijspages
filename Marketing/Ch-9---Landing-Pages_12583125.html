To access Landing Pages from the dashboard, navigate to Content > Landing Pages. Please note, you can also read HS’s own guidance on this subject here.

The presented landing page dashboard will have opened under the ‘Manage’ tab, which displays all currently available landing page designs, both published and drafted. You can distinguish between them, as published pages will display a small green circle, and drafted pages will display a small grey circle.

You can sort the view of the ‘Manage’ tab, by selecting either Page Name, Last Updated, or Created Date.

In addition, there are three other menus to take note of, which:

  • All landing pages: Sorts between drafts, published, scheduled, and archived pages

  • All campaigns: Sort between campaigns

  • Folders: Sort between folders

To manage these pages there are some hidden options available to the left of the ‘created date’ for a website entry, hover your cursor over this section to view buttons for Edit, Clone, and More.

Changing the dashboard to the ‘Analyse’ function (below), will now show the associated metric data for these pages, all of which can be sorted by the options for Submissions, New Customers, and Customers, all of which can be cross-referenced by ‘All time’.

How to change a Landing Page into a Website Page:

To change a landing page into a website page (for whatever reason), click the dropdown menu for ‘more’ (located towards the far right) and choose ‘Move to website pages’.

A popup will then appear and state a condition of the move, if okay, press the ‘Move to Website Pages’ button to finish.
