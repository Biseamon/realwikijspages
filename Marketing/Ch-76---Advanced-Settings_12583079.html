Blueberry : Ch. 7.6 - Advanced Settings

Created by Stelios Dritsas on Jul 01, 2016

On the same page as Settings complete the following information:

  1. Template settings: You should not need to change your template, but you may swap or edit your template from here. Please note, editing your template may also affect other templates already published, so please consider the ramifications before proceeding.

  2. Footer:

    1. Office location: Use the default office location, or manage/edit the details by selecting the manage button. From here you can make changes or create a new set.

    2. Footer information: Editing footer information is similar to the emails template, in that any changes made will also carry across to other published emails. So please consider the ramifications before proceeding.

Once you’ve completed these steps, press save and proceed to the ‘Recipients’ tab.
