Blueberry : Ch. 9.2 - Forms

Created by Stelios Dritsas, last modified by Unknown User (mct) on Jul 07, 2016

There are a few extra points to cover if you’re using a form within your landing page design.

Firstly, if you need to collect new contact information that an existing form does not gather, then you will need to create a new form. Please see chapter 4 for instructions. When your new form is ready, there are three ways you can insert it into a landing page design:

  1. Clone a landing page and swap its existing form for your newly created form, in addition to editing all its content and settings - suggested method.

  2. Create a new landing page template and insert your new form into its design, then develop a landing page from this. Please see chapter 12 for instructions.

  3. Clone an existing landing page template and swap its existing form for your newly created form, then develop a landing page from this. Please see chapter 12 for instructions.

Additional Actions: To edit a landing page’s form, click on its module within the ‘Edit’ tab and complete the following steps from within the presented box.

  1. Form title: Choose an actionable title that clarifies what the form offers

  2. Form: Use the existing form, swap for another, or create a new form

    1. For instructions on how to create a Form, see chapter 4.

  3. Visitor redirection: Choose where you want to send your visitors after they sign-up

    1. Redirect to another page - send sign-ups to an existing thank you page (see chapter 8 for how to create new pages)

    2. Display and inline thank you – this displays a message of your choosing on the current page

    3. Out of the two options above, the most optimized method is to redirect your sign-up users to an alternative thank you page

  4. Form submission notifications: Have a notification email sent out when your form is completed, add you chosen email addresses here

  5. Add to workflow: When a contact completes your form have them added to an existing workflow, or create a new one (see chapter 10 for how create a workflow)

  6. Send a follow-up email: When a contact completes your form have them sent a specific email, or create a new one (see chapter 7 for how create an email)

Once you’ve completed these steps, press save and proceed to the ‘Settings’ tab.
