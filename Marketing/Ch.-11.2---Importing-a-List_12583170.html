Blueberry : Ch. 11.2 - Importing a List

Created by Stelios Dritsas on Jul 04, 2016

To import a list into HubSpot, head to the list’s dashboard and click the import button located towards the top right of the page (below).

From the opened page press the blue ‘Import contacts’ button, and follow the steps below:

  1. Choose a CSV file and press upload.

  2. Choose a name for your list then press ‘Next, map your properties’.

  3. Match the column labels from your CSV to the relevant HubSpot properties, then press next.

  4. Confirm the validity of your data by selecting the true options from the radio boxes, then confirm your initials and press import contacts.

  5. You’ll then have a slight delay before your contacts are fully imported.
