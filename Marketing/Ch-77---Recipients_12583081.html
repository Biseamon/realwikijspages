Blueberry : Ch. 7.7 - Recipients

Created by Stelios Dritsas on Jul 01, 2016

From the Recipients tab (towards the top of the page) you can choose who to send your email to or save your email for automation to be used in workflows.

By default, HubSpot will have the ‘add recipients’ check box selected at first, but if your email is for automation (workflow use) then go ahead and select ‘automation’ (below).

If you wish to add recipients, you can do so by either selecting the tab for ‘List’ or by ‘Individual contacts’ (below)

From the Lists tab you can scroll down the lists available and choose your list, or if you know the name of your list enter it into the search bar.

For Individual contacts, you’ll need to enter the name of your contacts separately, so make sure to have them to hand.

When you proceed to select a list or contact for sending, by hovering over the entry HubSpot will provide you with the options for Include or Exclude. For example, if you have two lists with contacts shared across both, by including one but excluding the other, contacts in the excluded list would also be removed from sending even if they were part of the Include list.

Once a contact or list has been selected, its details will appear in either the ‘Send to’ or ‘Don’t send to’ boxes to right. If you wish to remove any contacts, select their name from theses boxes and click the ‘X’ button to remove them.

Once you’ve completed these steps, press save and proceed to the ‘Review tab.
