To access Emails from the dashboard, navigate to Content > Email. Note: Please note, you can also read HS’s own guidance on this subject here.

The page presented shows recent email performance, and from here you can get a quick snap-shot of how your email campaigns are currently shaping up. To sort your view, use the drop-down menus for All Emails and All Campaigns to filter presented emails, alongside the buttons for Recent and Monthly to change the time period. This page also displays what recent drafts emails have been edited, in addition to any recent email contact churn (a natural part of email marketing).
