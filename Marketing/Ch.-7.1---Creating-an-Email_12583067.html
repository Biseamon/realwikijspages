Blueberry : Ch. 7.1 - Creating an Email

Created by Stelios Dritsas, last modified by Unknown User (mct) on Jul 07, 2016

To create an email, you can either clone an existing email or create a new one from scratch. To clone an email, find your preferred email, pan to the right, find the ‘gear’ icon, click clone from drop-down menu to its right, and enter a new name. Or to create a new email, click the blue button located towards the top right-hand side of the page called ‘Create new email’.

The presented page will then ask you to choose a template for your email - look to utilise one with the preferred header and/or footer image, alongside the necessary module layout. For CMM emails I mostly use the template ‘CMM 1 Column Above Body’, but you can preview other designs by hovering over the template and selecting the magnifying glass.

To create a new email template see chapter 12 for instructions.
