To create a HubSpot template navigate to Content and click > Design Manager. Please note, you can read HS’s own guidance on this subject here.

Unlike HubSpot’s other features, the design manager allows you to view editable templates through ‘page tabs’ rather than having just a singular page open for editing.

As such, when you open the design manager from the main dashboard it will subsequently direct to your last open page (alongside any other open templates), rather than forwarding you to an overview page. If you haven’t worked upon a page previously, then it will simply open a ‘New tab’ and display a default view for ‘Recently Edited’ templates.

To find a specific template you can either search through the left-hand menu folders (shown below), or enter your templates name into the search bar. When found, click on your template to be forwarded to its editing tab.
